The Funding Game
Startup simulation

Raise money without losing your company.

In this prototype, you play as the founder of Startech, a growing startup that needs capital to survive and scale. Your challenge is to choose investors, negotiate smartly, and manage the long-term trade-off between growth, control, and ownership.

What this prototype tests
Core mechanism
Choose funding type, deal terms, and negotiation strategy across 3 rounds.
Learning alignment
Understand dilution, investor incentives, and long-term consequences.
Competition
Finish with a score and compare yourself on the leaderboard.
