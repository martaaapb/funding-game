The Funding Game
Startup simulation · v2

Raise money without losing your company.

You play as the founder of Startech. Balance cash, growth, ownership, and investor trust across three rounds — plus unpredictable market events.

🎯 Scoring is transparent Every stat has a weight. Growth (35%), Ownership (30%), Cash (20%), Trust (15%). You'll see your live score at all times.
📉 Dilution compounds Each round where you give up equity is tracked visually. Drop below 50% and you start losing strategic control.
🎲 Random events Each round may trigger a market event that changes conditions before you choose. Adapt your strategy.
